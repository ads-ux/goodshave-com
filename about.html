About GoodShave.com

Independent razor testing since 2021. No paid placements. No brand relationships. Just honest reviews from people who shave every day.

Who We Are

GoodShave.com is an independent review publication dedicated to helping men find the best razor for their face without the marketing noise. We're not affiliated with any razor brand. We buy all razors ourselves and test them over several weeks before publishing any recommendation.

The site was founded in 2021 by a group of wet shaving enthusiasts who were frustrated with review sites that seemed to rank products based on affiliate commission rates rather than actual quality.

How We Test

Every razor reviewed on GoodShave.com goes through our standardized testing protocol before receiving a score. Here's exactly how we evaluate:

CriteriaMethodWeight
Shave ClosenessMeasured 4 hours post-shave via touch test on 3 face zones30%
Irritation ScoreVisual redness + reported sensation on a 1–5 scale, 3 skin types25%
Blade LongevityAverage shaves per blade before noticeable performance drop20%
Value (Price/Shave)Cost per shave calculated at average longevity15%
Handle & ErgonomicsGrip in wet conditions, balance, pivot mechanism10%

Each razor is tested over a minimum of 3 weeks across at least 2 testers with different skin types. We never accept free products from brands in exchange for reviews.

Our Affiliate Relationship Policy

GoodShave.com earns affiliate commissions when you purchase through our links. This is how we fund the site. However, our policy is strict: we will never rank a product higher or lower based on commission rate. Our rankings are locked before any affiliate links are applied.
