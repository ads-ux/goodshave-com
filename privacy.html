Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

GoodShave.com collects minimal personal information. The only information we collect is:

  • Email addresses — only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter via the sign-up form on our site.
  • Analytics data — anonymous page view data collected via Google Analytics (IP addresses are anonymized). This helps us understand which content is useful.
  • Cookie data — affiliate tracking cookies placed by our affiliate partners (Amazon Associates, Impact.com, etc.) when you click our links. These cookies do not identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Email addresses are used only to send the newsletter you signed up for. We never sell or rent email lists.
  • Analytics data is used to improve site content and user experience.
  • We do not use your information for targeted advertising.

3. Affiliate Links & Cookies

GoodShave.com participates in affiliate programs. When you click a product link, an affiliate cookie may be placed on your browser by the retailer (Amazon, Dollar Shave Club, Harry's, etc.). If you make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no extra cost to you. You can opt out of affiliate tracking by using a browser extension that blocks tracking cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services on this site:

  • Google Analytics — anonymized traffic analytics
  • Google Fonts — font delivery (Playfair Display, Inter)
  • Impact.com — affiliate tracking platform
  • Unsplash — image delivery (CDN)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them separately.

5. Data Retention

Newsletter email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained for 26 months (Google Analytics default). We do not store payment information — all purchases happen on the retailers' own websites.

6. Your Rights

If you are in the EU/UK, you have rights under GDPR/UK GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights or to unsubscribe from our newsletter, email hello@goodshave.com.

7. Children's Privacy

GoodShave.com is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@goodshave.com.
