Why We Picked It

The Series 9 Pro+ is the most capable electric foil shaver we tested on tough, multi-directional stubble — its 5-element head and AutoSense pressure-adjusting motor handled coarse necklines with almost no tugging. It's also fully wet/dry, so you can run it with foam in the shower if you want a hybrid routine, or dry for a quick morning shave. The tradeoff is price: at nearly triple the cost of budget electric shavers, it's an investment, not an impulse buy.

Common complaints center on cost and refinements around the edges rather than the core shave: the slide-out pop-up trimmer is frequently described as mediocre and needs multiple passes on sideburns, the shaver runs louder than some rotary competitors, and on very dense or coarse beards it doesn't quite close the gap with Panasonic's Arc line. The newest 2025 revision (96xx series) added Titanium Nitride-coated blades and a faster-scanning pressure sensor, and remains backward-compatible with older Series 9 units.

Who It's For — and Who Should Skip It

Get this if: you shave daily, want the closest possible electric shave without picking up a blade, and are willing to pay a premium for it. The SmartCare cleaning station is genuinely convenient if you want a low-maintenance routine.

Skip it if: budget matters more than closeness — the Andis ReSurge covers the basics for a fraction of the price. If you have very coarse, dense facial hair and closeness is your top priority over comfort, also compare the Panasonic Arc5, which several reviewers rate slightly ahead on raw cutting power.

Common Questions

Is the Braun Series 9 Pro+ worth the price over a budget shaver?+
If closeness and comfort on sensitive skin are priorities, most reviewers say yes. If you mainly want a functional daily shave and cost matters more, a budget foil shaver like the Andis ReSurge covers the basics for much less.
How long does the battery actually last?+
About 60 minutes of shaving per full charge, with a 5-minute quick charge giving roughly one shave — useful in a pinch.
Is the pop-up trimmer any good?+
It's the weakest part of the package — reviewers consistently describe it as needing multiple passes on sideburns and mustache detail, not a substitute for a dedicated trimmer.
What's the difference between Series 9, Series 9 Pro, and Pro+?+
Series 9 is the baseline; Pro (94xx, 2021) added the ProLift trimmer for longer growth; Pro+ (95xx, 2023 and the newer 96xx, 2025) redesigned the trimmer again and added a faster pressure sensor and Titanium Nitride blades on the latest revision.
